# 行为准则 / Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

我们作为社区的贡献者和维护者，承诺让每个人不受骚扰，无论年龄、体型、残疾、种族、经验水平、教育程度、社会地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性认同和性取向如何。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

有助于促进社区开放和温馨环境的行为示例：

- 对他人的经验表示尊重和同理心
- 尊重不同的经验、观点和经历
- 愿意接受建设性批评并从中学习
- 关注对社区整体最有利的事情
- 对其他社区成员展现同理心

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

有害行为的示例：

- 使用性化的语言或图像，以及性关注或挑逗
- 挑衅、侮辱性或贬损的评论，以及人身或政治攻击
- 公开或私下的骚扰行为
- 未经明确许可公布他人的私人信息（如实体地址或电子邮件地址）
- 其他可以被合理认为不恰当的行为

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 我们的责任 / Our Responsibilities

社区维护者负责澄清可接受的行为标准，并采取适当公平的纠正措施应对任何不可接受的行为。

社区维护者有权且有责任删除、编辑或拒绝不符合本行为准则的评论、提交、代码、Wiki 编辑、问题和其他贡献，并在适当时说明管理决策的缘由。

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## 适用范围 / Scope

本行为准则适用于社区空间以及公共空间当个人代表社区时。

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## 执行 / Enforcement

如有其他不当行为的报告，请联系项目维护者 jadepam。所有投诉将被及时审查和调查。

All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

Reports should be directed to jadepam, the project maintainer. We participate in [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
